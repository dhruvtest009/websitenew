Echoes of Melancholy

In the echoes of our old conversations,
I find a melancholy melody,
A symphony of the way I used to think of you,
A ballad of lost butterflies and silent pleas.

In the soft glow of our texts, now silent,
Words linger like ghosts of laughter
The cadence of our shared jokes,
Fading into the hollow spaces of my heart.

Your smile, once a sunbeam that danced,
Now a distant memory, a ghostly waltz,
Gave birth to butterflies that fluttered,
Now trapped in the cage of time's ruthless grasp

Silent Promises

They say sometimes we miss the bond,
Not the person, yet here I am,
Longing for the echo of your laughter,
The ghost of your presence, haunting my soul.

I sacrificed sleep on the altar of our connection,
A nocturnal offering to the gods of love,
Yet in the darkness, I found only emptiness,
A void that no amount of words could fill.

Eagerly waiting for a reply that never came,
I stood on the shores of hope,
Watching the waves of anticipation,
Crash against the cliffs of reality.

Linger in Shadows

The past, an unyielding anchor,
Refusing to release its grip,
No matter how I try to sail away,
I am tethered to the memory of you.

No matter how far I journey in life,
Your ghost accompanies me,
A silent companion in the theater of my mind,
Where the curtains never fall on our shared scenes.

One-sided feelings,a bittersweet potion,
Both sipping from the cup of illusion,
But now, the cup lies shattered,
Its fragments cutting through the fabric of what once was.

And so, I linger in the shadows of what was,
A poet of sorrow, a storyteller of lost dreams,
In the chapters of us, I find solace,
In the verses of pain, I find a sad, sweet release.
